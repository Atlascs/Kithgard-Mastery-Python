## The Long Kithmaze
## As it says you must repeat it twice.

# This is a long maze...
self.moveRight()
self.moveRight()
self.moveUp()
self.moveUp()

# Now you'll have to repeat the above steps two more times to get to the end of the maze...
self.moveRight()
self.moveRight()
self.moveUp()
self.moveUp()
self.moveRight()
self.moveRight()
self.moveUp()
self.moveUp()
